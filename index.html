Connect 4 Rules

  • Players will take turns to drop one of their tokens into one of the columns.
  • The first player to connect four of their tokens in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally wins.
  • If all slots are filled and no player has four in a row, the game ends in a draw.
  • Click the 'Reset Game' button to start a new round!
